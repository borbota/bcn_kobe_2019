Barcelona – people and resources

Barcelona is a metropolis, which has to serve its ~1.6 million inhabitants and millions of tourists. If we think about climate change in such a city, what do we think of? Are there parks to provide shelter on the hot summer days? Is water available for everyone? Are there cars polluting in the neighborhood? Are there many tourists increasing the abuse of the land? In the charts below, you can check the total number of these resources on the right, while on the left the same metrics per 1000 inhabitants by each district.
