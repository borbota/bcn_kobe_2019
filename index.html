Barcelona – people and resources


Barcelona has to serve its ~1.6 million inhabitants and millions of tourists. If we think about climate change in such a city, what do we think of? Are there parks to provide shelter on the hot summer days? Is water available for everyone? Are there cars polluting in the neighborhood? Are there many tourists increasing the abuse of the land? In the charts below you can see the total number of these resources on the left, while on the right the same resources per 1000 inhabitants by each district, use the drop-down menu to switch between the metrics.
